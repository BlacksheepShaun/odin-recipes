Mac and Cheese

Simple and tasty mac and cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 box (8 ounces) of elbow macaroni
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Chedder cheese

Steps

  1. Boil Macaroni
  2. Make a Roux
  3. Drain the Macaroni
  4. Add Cheese and Macaroni

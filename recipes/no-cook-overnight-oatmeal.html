No-cook Overnight Oatmeal

Delicious overnight oatmeal

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup milk
  • ¼ cup rolled oats
  • ¼ cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons chia seeds
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup fresh blueberries

Steps

  1. Put milk, oats, Greek yogurt, chia seeds, honey, and cinnamon in a 1/2-pint jar
  2. Cover the lid and shake until combined
  3. Remove the lid and top with blueberry
  4. Put it in the refrigerator for overnight
